About Waggy Box

Dogs are extremely important to us. We love ours, and we want to be able to share that love too! All the treats and toys in our boxes are naturally produced, and guaranteed to be loved by your 4-legged best friend!

What's more, your subscription goes further than just your own dog! By sigining up to the monthly subscription, every time you recieve a box, a doggy with no home gets a box too! Each month, we pick one animal shelter located anywhere in the world and send out a box for every box bought!
